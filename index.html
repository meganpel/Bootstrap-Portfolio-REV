About

Megan Pellegrino-Zubricky, MLA is a writer, photographer, videographer, and designer with experience in creating media for both print and web. She is skilled and trained in various editing and media outlets including Final Cut Pro, Motion, Logic, and Soundtrack Pro, with a high level experience using Bridge, Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop.

Megan received her Bachelor of Arts degree from Cabrini University in English and Communication and a Master of Liberal Arts from the University of Pennsylvania with a focus in Creative Writing and Urban Studies. She has also taken non-degree coursework at the University of Pennsylvania in Spanish, Photography, and Cinema Studies.

Megan is currently enrolled in a Certificate Full Stack Coding Boot Camp at the University of Pennsylvania where she will receive her certificate in October 2018. She is proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Firebase, Node, MySQL, Express, Handlebars and Sequelize.
