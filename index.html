About

Megan Pellegrino-Zubricky, MLA is the Multimedia Manager at the University of Pennsylvania's Leonard Davis Institute of Health Economics. As the Multimedia Manager, she manages, edits and maintains the renowned research institute's portal website. She also co-manages the multimedia production of all digital and web content including, video, audio, newsletter, email networks, blog as well as social media systems. Megan is highly experienced and trained in various editing and media outlets including Final Cut Pro, Motion, Bridge, Illustrator, InDesign, and Photoshop.

Megan received her Bachelor of Arts degree from Cabrini University in English and Communication and a Master of Liberal Arts from the University of Pennsylvania with a focus in Creative Writing and Urban Studies. She has also taken non-degree coursework at the University of Pennsylvania in Spanish, Photography, and Cinema Studies. Megan is currently enrolled in a Coding Boot Camp at the University of Pennsylvania. She will receive her Certificate in October 2018.
